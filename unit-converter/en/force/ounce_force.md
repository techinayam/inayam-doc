## Ounce Force (ozf) Converter Tool

### Definition
The ounce force (ozf) is a unit of force that represents the force exerted by one ounce of mass under standard gravity. It is commonly used in various fields, including engineering, physics, and material science, to quantify force in a more accessible manner. Understanding the ounce force is crucial for accurate measurements in applications ranging from mechanical systems to everyday tasks.

### Standardization
The ounce force is standardized based on the gravitational force acting on a mass of one ounce at sea level, which is approximately 9.81 m/s². This standardization allows for consistent calculations and conversions across different measurement systems, ensuring that users can rely on the accuracy of their results when using the ounce force converter.
