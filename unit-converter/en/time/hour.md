### Definition

An hour is defined as a period of time equivalent to 60 minutes or 3,600 seconds. It is one of the standard units of time used globally, making it a fundamental aspect of timekeeping.

### Standardization

The hour is standardized in the International System of Units (SI) as a unit of time. It is universally accepted across various fields, including science, engineering, and everyday life, ensuring consistency in time measurement.

### History and Evolution

The concept of the hour dates back to ancient civilizations, where sundials were used to divide the day into segments. The division of the day into 24 hours was established by the Egyptians and later adopted by the Greeks and Romans. Over centuries, the hour has evolved, but its significance remains unchanged in our modern world.
