## Fluid Ounce Converter Tool

### Definition
The fluid ounce (symbol: fl oz) is a unit of volume commonly used in the United States and the United Kingdom. It is primarily utilized to measure liquids and is an essential unit in various fields such as cooking, nutrition, and beverage production. Understanding fluid ounces is crucial for accurate measurements in recipes, dietary guidelines, and product labeling.

### Standardization
In the United States, one fluid ounce is equivalent to approximately 29.57 milliliters, while in the UK, it is defined as about 28.41 milliliters. This distinction is important for users who may be converting recipes or nutritional information across different regions. Our fluid ounce converter tool allows you to easily switch between these units and ensures accurate conversions for your needs.

### History and Evolution
The fluid ounce has its roots in the medieval period, evolving from the measurements of various liquid capacities used in trade and commerce. Over time, different countries adopted their own standards, leading to the variations we see today. The standardization of fluid ounces has been crucial in facilitating international trade and communication, especially in the food and beverage industry.
