### 예제 계산

\[ \text{Angular Acceleration} = \frac{\Delta \text{Angular Velocity}}{\Delta \text{Time}} \]

\[ \text{Angular Acceleration} = \frac{300 \, \text{rev/min} - 100 \, \text{rev/min}}{5 \, \text{s}} = \frac{200 \, \text{rev/min}}{5 \, \text{s}} = 40 \, \text{rev/min²} \]
